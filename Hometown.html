Vita - The City Of Gold

Vita is a city and a municipal council in Sangli district in Maharashtra. Vita city is also known as City Of Gold beacause of gold jwellers in nearby areas. Vita was ranked 9th in both the west zone and the state for Best City in 'Innovation & Best Practices' in 2020. Vita is well known for festival celebrations.The biggest and best being Dussera and Ganpati. In ganpati festival Vityacha Raja is the biggest public ganpati idol which is very famous in all nearby areas for the idol and decoration and people have huge belif for god Ganpati. On dusserra palakhis of Revansiddha and Bhairavnatha compete against each other. Also, one national and one state highway passes through Vita.
